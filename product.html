Story about the product

The product that we are promoting is the virtial assisting referee also known as the VAR. This product exist of multiple different products. First of all we have the cameras, these camera's are placed all arround the sport field to capture every moment from every angle, then we have the computers that get sent the footage from the cameras and have programs to analyse the footage, these programs are used to digitally analye the footage and see what truely happened at the sporting field.

What problem does this product solve

the main problem this product solves is when the players or the coaches are not agreeing with the referees decision on a certain matter for example a foul or offside in football or an situation in baseball if the player is safe or out. The reason this product solves this problem is the fact that you can rewatch te situation and from different angles in contrast with the moment it happens where you can only see it once and only from one angle

How to use this innovation

The way you use this innovation. To use this innovation you first need to install the cameras around the field in places so that every part of the field is captured from multiple points of view. then you need to install the computers on which you can watch and analyse the footage and then show it to the referee. And last of all you need to install the progams with which you can analyse and check the footage taken from the camera. If all this is done you are all set to use this product

who is using this innovation

This innovation is already being used all over the world on a bigger scale. This product is being used by almost every pro football league, for example the Premier League and La Liga. During football games it is used to see if a foul was as bad as the referee called it or if a play is offside or not. But it is not only used in football it is used in a lot of different sport, like baseball where it is used to see if the umpire made the right call by saying the player is out or if the player is safe.

What can you expect from us when we are delivering our product

You can expect a number of things. First of all you can expect us to deliver everything you need to set up your own VAR-system for you sportingfield. You can expect all your items to be delivered in two weeks after your order. When you receive the products they come with clear instructions on how to install the cameras and the computer, but if you want help with installing and placing the cameras you can ask for someone from our company to come and help you out with no extra costs.

The future for this innovation

We think this innovation has a big future ahead of it. We think this because until now this product is almost only used in professional sport leagues, and with our product we can get the VAR to be used in smaller and local leagues to improve these games. This product also makes ways to rewatch certain moments in a sports game for fans of the sport to rewatch after the game. Because of these reasons we think that this innovation will be helpfull in the future.
